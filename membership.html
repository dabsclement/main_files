As you probably know, in some parts of the world young girls will often use sanitary towels (usually scrap cloth) in place of a sanitary pad. Of course they are washed thoroughly between uses, but venereal diseases can be transmitted through the process of washing and reusing. Also, study as shown that most of these underprivileged girls miss school for at least four days each month over menstruation. These issues are not talked about in some countries, due to embarrassment, lack of understanding and the low priority of such problems in the face of starvation, violence, death, illiteracy and the many struggles of daily life.
